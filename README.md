## Viva Answers
**What is Lead Time?**
Lead time is the total time from placing an order to receiving
the product. It includes supplier processing, manufacturing,
and shipping time. Lower lead time = better supply chain efficiency.

**Explain the Bullwhip Effect.**
The Bullwhip Effect is when small changes in consumer demand
cause increasingly larger fluctuations in orders up the supply chain.
Example: A 10% increase in retail sales causes a 40% increase
in manufacturer orders, leading to overproduction and excess inventory.
